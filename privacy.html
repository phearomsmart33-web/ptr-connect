Privacy Policy

Customer information is collected for service and verification purposes.

We collect only the information needed to review requests, confirm supplier services, issue itemized invoices, support payment processing, and maintain compliance records.

Information We May Collect

  • Name, contact details, nationality, and travel or case information.
  • Identity documents for KYC review when required.
  • Invoice, receipt, supplier booking reference, and service communication records.
  • Payment status from the approved gateway. Raw card data is not stored by this website.

How Information Is Used

  • To confirm supplier availability and prepare customer invoices.
  • To support AML/KYC review for high-value or higher-risk requests.
  • To resolve refunds, disputes, chargebacks, and service questions.
  • To maintain business records required by partners, processors, or law.

Data Sharing

Customer information may be shared with selected hotels, tour operators, professional suppliers, payment processors, banks, or professional advisers only when needed for service delivery, payment handling, compliance, or dispute resolution.
