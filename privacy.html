Privacy Policy

Customer information is used only for legitimate service needs.

PRATHNA KASEKOR KHMER IMPORT EXPORT CO., LTD. collects the information needed to review requests, coordinate confirmed services, issue invoices, support secure payment and maintain appropriate business records.

  • Name, email, phone number, nationality and passenger information.
  • Travel dates, destinations, accommodation, transport and special requirements.
  • Identity or supporting documents when verification is required.

  • Payment status received from the approved payment provider.

How information is used

  • To verify the customer and coordinate requested services.
  • To confirm supplier availability and prepare invoices.
  • To prevent fraud and resolve questions, refunds, disputes or chargebacks.
  • To meet legal, accounting, banking or payment-partner requirements.

Sharing and protection

Information may be shared only when needed with selected service suppliers, banks, payment providers or professional advisers. PRATHNA KASEKOR KHMER IMPORT EXPORT CO., LTD. does not sell customer data and does not store raw card numbers, CVV, PIN or OTP information.

Contact and correction

Customers may contact PRATHNA KASEKOR KHMER IMPORT EXPORT CO., LTD. to request correction of inaccurate information or ask a privacy question, subject to records that must be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.
