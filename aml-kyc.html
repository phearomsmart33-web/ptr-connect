AML / KYC Procedure

Verification controls for safe service payments.

AML/KYC checks help confirm that payment requests are connected to real supplier services, customer identity records, itemized invoices, and agency service records.

Standard Review

  • Record customer name, contact details, service purpose, and requested dates.
  • Confirm supplier availability, price, and service scope.
  • Issue invoice with service description, supplier cost, agency fee, amount, and cancellation terms.
  • Keep receipt, supplier booking proof, and communication records.

Enhanced Review

High-value cases, unusual requests, inconsistent customer details, or payments that do not match the service purpose may require additional identity verification, source-of-funds explanation, management approval, or rejection.

Prohibited Requests

  • Anonymous payments or payments without an itemized service invoice.
  • Payments intended for cash-out, remittance, card-to-card transfer, debt collection, or unsupported transfers.
  • Requests involving false documents, false identity, or unclear service purpose.
