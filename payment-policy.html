Payment Policy

Visa and Mastercard payments for approved agency invoices only.

PTR Connect acts as an agency coordinator. Customer invoices may include third-party supplier costs plus the agency service fee, shown clearly before payment. Payments are processed only through an approved payment gateway or merchant acquirer; PTR Connect does not operate card-to-card transfers, cash-out, remittance, or unsupported money movement.

Payment Conditions

  • Payment is requested only after supplier availability and scope are confirmed.
  • Each transaction must be linked to an approved itemized invoice and customer record.
  • High-value cases may require identity verification and additional review.
  • Visa or Mastercard payments must be authorized by the approved gateway and settled to the merchant account.
  • Supplier booking or service work proceeds only after payment authorization is approved.
  • A receipt or payment confirmation is issued after successful payment.

Not Accepted

  • Anonymous payments.
  • Unsupported transfer requests without a confirmed service purpose and itemized invoice.
  • Payments for third-party cash disbursement, cash-out, or debt collection.
  • Card-to-card, person-to-person, or remittance-style payment activity.
  • Card payments that do not match the customer, invoice, or service record.

Secure Checkout

The live payment gateway should be configured through an approved merchant acquirer or payment processor using hosted checkout or another approved secure payment link. Card authentication such as Visa Secure and Mastercard Identity Check should be enabled where available. This website does not store raw card numbers.
