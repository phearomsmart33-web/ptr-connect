Refund Policy

Clear cancellation and refund terms before payment.

Refund eligibility depends on service type, supplier confirmation status, agency work already performed, and cancellation timing. The final invoice may include more specific terms for each request.

Before Service Confirmation

If payment was collected and the requested service cannot be confirmed by the supplier, the customer may receive a refund according to processor and bank timelines. Any agency fee treatment should follow the invoice terms accepted by the customer.

After Service Confirmation

  • Hotel bookings follow the real hotel or supplier cancellation policy.
  • Tour packages may include non-refundable agency coordination, reservation, or supplier costs.
  • Business consulting and local legal coordination fees may be non-refundable once agency or supplier work begins.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method where possible. Processing time depends on the card issuer, acquirer, and payment gateway.
